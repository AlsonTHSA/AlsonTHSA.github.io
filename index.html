I am currerntly serving my nation service, i enlisted on 1st October so its been about 2 and a half months into my service. However, serving Nation Service is mandatory for every Singaporean men, so in a way i am proud that i am able to keep up with what majority of Singaporean men has done. However, i can't help but feel sad that i am not able to pursue what i want to do in life and have to be pushed behind 2 years before i can really get my life started.
